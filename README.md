Decoding Digital Influence: A Data-Driven Analysis of Instagram Engagement Dynamics

A comprehensive analytical dashboard exploring patterns behind influencer engagement, audience behavior, and content performance on Instagram.

# Overview

This project investigates how influencers drive audience engagement on Instagram using data-driven insights.
By integrating quantitative metrics (likes, comments, sentiment) with qualitative behavioral understanding (emotion, timing, and niche impact), this study reveals actionable strategies for creators and marketers to optimize their content and community growth.

The interactive dashboard visualizes engagement trends across multiple dimensions ‚Äî emotion, post type, timing, hashtags, virality, and influencer fields.

# Key Objectives

Analyze factors influencing engagement (likes + comments).

Identify field-specific trends among influencers (Sports, Music, Lifestyle, etc.).

Examine sentiment and emotion effects on audience response.

Evaluate posting time patterns for optimal visibility.

Compare post formats (video, carousel, image) to determine performance drivers.

Highlight top influencers by average engagement and comment-to-like ratio.

# Features of the Dashboard

1. Temporal Heatmaps: Identify engagement peaks by day and hour.

2. Emotion & Sentiment Analysis: Visualize how positivity and tone affect engagement.

3. Post Type Comparison: Explore how videos, carousels, and images perform across niches.

4.  Hashtag Efficiency Map: Discover the relation between tag frequency and reach.

5. Correlation Matrix: Understand interdependencies between likes, comments, and virality.

6. Influencer Ranking: Dynamic ranking of creators by engagement and authenticity.
